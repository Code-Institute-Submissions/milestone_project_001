Red Kyte

My latest piece of work is this Red Kyte. I was asked to make it for a lady who had lost her husband. She wanted a sculpture to put his ashes in and as they both had a love of bird watching and especially Red Kytes I designed this piece. It is destined to sit in her garden as it is weatherproof.
